## Full Description
Tsewa brings intention back to dating. Inspired by the Tibetan concept of loving-kindness, Tsewa helps you find meaningful connections rooted in shared values, genuine conversation, and mutual respect.

Unlike swipe-driven apps that reduce people to split-second judgments, Tsewa encourages you to slow down, reflect, and connect with people on a deeper level.

**What makes Tsewa different:**

- Value-based matching: Get paired with people who share what matters most to you
- Thoughtful prompts: Conversation starters designed to spark real dialogue, not small talk
- Intentional pacing: Features that encourage getting to know someone before moving on
- Multilingual support: Connect with people across languages and cultures
- Privacy-first: Your data stays yours — we never sell it to advertisers

**How it works:**

1. Create your profile and share what you value most
2. Answer reflective prompts that reveal your personality
3. Get matched with compatible people based on values, not just photos
4. Start conversations with built-in prompts that go beyond "hey"
5. Build connections at your own pace

Tsewa is for anyone seeking a partner who matches not just their interests, but their intentions.

Download Tsewa and start dating with purpose.

## Privacy Policy Requirements

### Data Collected
The following data is collected and linked to user identity:

**Contact Info**
- Email address (account creation, authentication)
- Phone number (optional, verification)

**User Content**
- Photos (profile pictures)
- Profile information (name, age, bio, prompts)
- Messages (in-app chat)

**Identifiers**
- User ID
- Device ID (analytics)

**Usage Data**
- App interactions (matching, messaging activity)
- Crash/performance data

**Location**
- Coarse location (city-level, for matching purposes)

### Data NOT Collected
- Precise GPS location
- Financial information
- Health/fitness data
- Browsing history
- Contacts/address book

### Data Sharing
- No data is sold to third parties
- No data shared with data brokers
- Analytics data may be shared with service providers (anonymized)

### Data Retention
- Account data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- Messages deleted when both parties delete the conversation
- Inactive accounts purged after 12 months of inactivity
